✦ WHO WE ARE

The Craft Behind Every Fabric

SAVITA SYNTHETICS is one of Surat's most trusted textile manufacturers, powered by 160–180 state-of-the-art waterjet looms running at full capacity. Based in the textile capital of India, we deliver consistent quality, bulk supply capabilities, and a fabric range that meets the demands of today's competitive market. Every meter we produce carries the promise of precision, durability, and craftsmanship.

✦ WHAT WE OFFER

Our Core Services

Waterjet Loom Manufacturing

Operating 160–180 high-speed waterjet looms, we manufacture premium-grade fabrics with exceptional consistency and output capacity.

Bulk Fabric Supply

We supply lakhs of meters monthly to wholesalers and retailers across Gujarat and India, ensuring reliable stock availability and consistent quality at every order scale.

Custom Fabric Production

Tailored fabric specifications — GSM, weave pattern, width, and finish — crafted to match your exact production requirements.

Cooperative & Local-First Approach

We believe in building lasting partnerships close to home. Our team is approachable, responsive and always ready to work with local traders, small businesses and regional buyers with the same dedication as large orders.
